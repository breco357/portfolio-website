My Design Journey

I'm Mike Hayden, based in Centennial, Colorado, the fourth sunniest state in the U.S.! I enjoy spending time with my wife & family, wrestling with our dogs, and playing the many guitars scattered around my house at random intervals throughout every day. (Inspiration strikes when it strikes, ya know?)

After moving deep into the Rocky Mountains to pursue a “professional” snowboarding career in my late teens, I attended the Colorado Institute of Art in downtown Denver. After working at a few small design houses and freelancing as a graphic designer, I found my way to the tech industry. From graphic designer to art director, UI designer to product designer, I spent the majority of my professional career working in marketing departments and on Product teams. These companies include MarketLeader, Trulia, Zillow, Constellation Software, and A2Z Sync.

Wearing many hats over the years had its advantages. Before I knew it, I had become a “human Swiss Army Knife”, taking on tasks ranging from print and digital design, identity and brand architecture, motion graphics and animation, illustration, UI design, website design, landing page and email development, and more.

My career path led me to discover Design Thinking principles and frameworks, and I've been obsessed with iterative data-driven decision-making ever since. After 5 years as a UI designer, I finally decided to switch things up and move into product design and, well, here we are!
