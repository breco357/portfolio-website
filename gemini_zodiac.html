Incorporating new UX for Gemini's Zodiac AI Assistant

Business: Gemini
Industry: Fintech
Role: Product Designer
Timeline: 3 weeks

Usability testing

Using rapid-prototyping for testing provided me quick insights to better position the Zodiac button where users expected it to be within the architecture.

Learnings: When fintech users want assistance they prefer not to have to dig for it in a list of menu items. They also expect an AI bot to function with exisiting patterns defined by products they are already familiar with.

Learning more at better fidelity

Further investigating whether or not the button will play well with the rest of the layout schema for the Gemini product.

Zodiac UX

Again, relying on exisiting AI interface patterns is important so users feel comfortable interacting with little to know learning curve.

Experimentation: I wanted to know if users would find chips for common prompts helpful or if it would be viewed as a disraction. I included sample prompts in this iteration for additional usability testing at higher fidelity.

Learnings: The inclusion of such prompts turned was received well and was viewed as a "nested" onboarding by those unfamiliar with fintech apps in general. This subtle feature was well received by both beginner and advanced users and will remain at this time.

Gemini Zodiak Summation

Usability testing proved to be very quick and affective while iterating at each stage of fidelity. Personalizing Zodiac by pulling the users' names dynamically from their profile proved to be a rewarding oportunity for individuals to establish a meaningful connection to the feature, and thus, to the Gemini product.
